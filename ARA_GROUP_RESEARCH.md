# ARA Group Research Summary

## Company Overview

**ARA Group** (ARA Group Limited, ABN 47 074 886 561) is an Australian, employee-owned company established in **2001** by co-founders **Leo Browne** and **Edward Federman**. The company is headquartered in **Crows Nest, New South Wales, Australia**.

### Mission & Tagline
- **Tagline**: "Here for you. Here for good."
- **Purpose**: Delivering fully integrated, in-house solutions and services for facilities and infrastructure projects across Australia and New Zealand.

### Company Structure
- **Type**: Employee-owned company (since 2007)
- **Employees**: Over 4,000 employees
- **Locations**: More than 100 locations across Australia and New Zealand
- **Geographic Presence**: Australia and New Zealand (expanded to NZ in 2016)
- **Revenue Growth**: 
  - **2020**: $621.3 million (10th consecutive year of record revenue)
  - **2022**: $721.4 million
  - **2023**: $884.9 million
  - **2024**: $969.0 million
  - **2025**: $1.204 billion (24% growth from 2024)

## Main Divisions & Organizations

### 1. **ARA Fire & Security**
- **Services**: High-quality integrated fire protection, security, and marine safety services
- **Capabilities**: Installation and maintenance of fire protection systems and electronic security systems
- **Website**: `fire.aragroup.com.au` and `arafireandsecurity.com`

### 2. **ARA Electrical**
- **Services**: Industrial low voltage and high voltage installation and service
- **Capabilities**: 
  - Electrical installation and construction
  - Electrical service and maintenance
  - Condition monitoring
  - Design services
  - Turnkey solutions for heavy industries
- **Website**: `electrical.aragroup.com.au`

### 3. **ARA Building Services**
- **Services**: Building maintenance and repair services
- **Capabilities**:

  - Remedial construction
  - Regular and emergency maintenance services
  - Installation and service of air conditioning and building automation systems
- **Website**: `buildingservices.aragroup.com.au`

### 4. **ARA Mechanical**

- **Capabilities**: Design, installation, and maintenance services

- **Website**: `mechanical.aragroup.com.au`

### 5. **ARA Property Services**

## Key Milestones & History

- **2001**: Company founded by Leo Browne and Edward Federman
- **2007**: Transitioned to employee-owned company
- **2016**: Expanded operations into New Zealand
- **2017**: 
  - Acquired CMC Property Services
  - Acquired 49% of CMC Indigenous Services and established ARA Indigenous Services
- **2019**: 
  - Achieved record revenue of $555 million
  - Continued expansion in New Zealand with acquisitions:
    - Neda Facilities Management (NFM)
    - Metalbilt
- **2020**: 10th consecutive year of record revenue, reaching $621.3 million
- **2022**: Revenue reached $721.4 million
- **2023**: Revenue reached $884.9 million; 22 years of sales and profitability
- **2024**: Revenue reached $969.0 million; Completed 23rd year of operations
- **2025**: Revenue reached $1.204 billion (24% growth); 24 years of operations
